About Us

Arm Glass & Mirror, is a well-established family-owned business with over 10 years experience providing glass services in GTA. With a considerable experience in Glass Service, We proudly assure our customers the Quality of work they will receive.

We manufacture and install custom Shower Doors, Partitions, Storefronts, Railings, Windows and Skylights, Table Tops, Wall Mirrors, Closet Doors, Shelving, and many other custom glass and mirror products. The understated beauty of glass will lend elegance to your home or commercial space, and mirrors carefully placed will open and brighten small urban enclosures.

When it comes to providing glass or mirror products for your home, Arm Glass & Mirror is your number one source as its Mission is to guarantee the customers best quality and price for any project.

Our Professionals are experts in the field of glass and mirror craftsmanship and provide the customers with the excellence in every project to enhance your home, cottages, decks and commercial buildings. Therefore, We are confident we will exceed your expectations in professionalism, innovation, quality workmanship, and unsurpassed customer service.

We offer FREE consultations and estimates, as well as 24/7 emergency service. Whether you're a homeowner or a business, Arm Glass & Mirror is the Custom Glass Company for You.
